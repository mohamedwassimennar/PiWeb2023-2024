Key Users

COACH

Monitor the development of your players, prepare training sessions and matches digitally, analyse and anticipate. Always be one step ahead of the opponents with the KickOff club management system. Keep track of all the affairs of your team in a structured way and communicate with the players and parents!

ASSISTANT COACH

Assist the head coach in various coaching activities, including training sessions, match preparation, and player development. Collaborate closely with the coaching staff to ensure the team's success.

PLAYER

As a player, actively participate in training sessions, matches, and team activities. Strive for continuous improvement and contribute to the team's success on and off the field.

DOCTOR

Provide medical support to players, diagnose injuries, and ensure the overall health and well-being of the team. Work closely with other medical staff to deliver optimal care.

PHYSIOTHERAPIST

Provide physiotherapy services to players, including injury rehabilitation, preventative exercises, and recovery treatments. Collaborate with medical and coaching staff to optimize player fitness and performance.

FITNESS COACH

Design and implement fitness programs tailored to individual players and team needs. Focus on improving physical conditioning, strength, and endurance to enhance performance and reduce injury risk.

TECHNICAL MANAGER

Oversee technical aspects of the team, including tactics, strategy, and player development. Coordinate with coaching staff to implement effective training programs and game plans.
